Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add courses?

Click the large green button above the timetable then input your CRN. Courses will only be added to your timetable once you click the submit button.

How do I save my timetable?

Saving is done automatically every time you add or remove a course.

Why aren't my courses saving?

Because Tables for UVic uses all the hot new technology saving may not be supported in older browsers. We use HTML5's localStorage feature to save data, so you'll need Chrome 4+, FireFox 3.5+, or IE8+. On mobile, you'll need iOS 3.2+, or Android 2.1+.

My web browser doesn't support localStorage, is there any other way I can save my timetable?

There is! After adding all your courses click the export link (top right) and bookmark that link. Upon loading that link again, Tables will automatically add the courses you had in your timetable when you had originally clicked the export link.

Do I have to type in all my CRNs on my phone?

You don't! Our export feature is designed to stop exactly this. Simply enter your CRNs on your computer then click 'Export' in the top right. Send this link to your phone (e.g. through email or Facebook) When you open that link on your phone all your courses will automatically be added.

How does the export feature work?

The export feature stores all courses in the URL. So, if you had added three courses with the CRNS 20001, 20051, and 20052, your URL would look like http://www.tablesforuvic.com/index.html?#20001#20051#20052.
